Molly Moon's Ice Cream

Local Seattle ice cream company that sources 90% of its ingredients from the Pacific Northwest. There are seven branches: Wallingford, U Village, Capitol Hill, Redmond, Madrona, Mercer, and Queen Anne.

Molly Moon offers 10 always flavors and 4 seasonal flavors. At time of posting, the Capitol Hill location offered the following always flavors: Vanilla Bean, Melted Chocolate, Strawberry, Scout Mint, Honey Lavender, Salted Caramel, Earl Grey, Stumptown Coffee, Maple Walnut, and Hillary Rodham Cookie Dough. The 4 seasonal flavors at this location were: Cinnamon, Raspberry RGB Sorbet, Ginger, and Vegan Melted Chocolate.

Pictured on left are Molly Moon's ginger and salted caramel ice creams. These servings were very affordable at around $5 per cup.

Address: 917 E Pine St, Seattle, WA 98122
