Molly Moon's Ice Cream

Local ice cream company that sources 90% of its ingredients from the Pacific Northwest. There are seven branches in Seattle, one of which is in Capitol Hill. Molly Moon has 10 always flavors (Vanilla Bean, Melted Chocolate, Strawberry, Scout Mint, Honey Lavender, Salted Caramel, Earl Grey, Stumptown Coffee, Maple Walnut, and Hillary Rodham Cookie Dough). Molly Moon also offers 4 seasonal flavors. The four seasonal flavors at the Capitol Hill location were: Cinnamon, Raspberry RGB Sorbet, Ginger, and Vegan Melted Chocolate.

Address: 917 E Pine St, Seattle, WA 98122
