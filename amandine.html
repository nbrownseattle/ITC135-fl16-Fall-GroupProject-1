Amandine's Bakery and Café

Amandine Bakeshop is a micro-bakery and café located in the Seattle Capitol Hill neighborhood. They specialize in regional French and classic English pastries that utilize classic pastry technique. Amandine offers a daily variety of macaron gerbet, or Parisian-style macarons. Amandine also showcases other regional macaron styles that have existed for centuries, highlighting the versatility of eggs, sugar, and almonds. You can also find a changing selection of viennoiseries, cookies, cakes, breakfast breads, savories, and tarts, made with their own handcrafted doughs in our open kitchen. The espresso drinks are made by Empire Espresso’s talented team.

Address: 1424 11th Ave, Seattle, WA 98122
