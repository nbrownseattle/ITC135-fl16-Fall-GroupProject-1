Ma'ono Fried Chicken & Whiskey

Located in West Seattle, Ma'ono is a restaurant that serves Hawaiian inspired dishes and craft cocktails. Ma'ono is a Hawaiian word that means "make delicious". They are famous for their fried chicken, served with sides of sweet hot sauce and kimchee. They also make a delicious saimin, with hand mande noodles in a rich pork broth. If you want to mix and match, they offer numerous small plates like musubi, poke, and manapua. The bar is stocked with over fourty different bourbons, ryes, and single malts. They also offer seasonal cocktails with house made bitters. Try a Knox Manhattan with cedar bitters, and a twist of orange.

4437 California Ave SW, Seattle, WA 98116
