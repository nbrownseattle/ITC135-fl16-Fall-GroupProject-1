Molly Moon's Ice Cream

Local Seattle ice cream company that sources 90% of its ingredients from the Pacific Northwest. There are seven branches: Wallingford, U Village, Capitol Hill, Redmond, Madrona, Mercer, and Queen Anne. Molly Moon offers 10 always flavors and 4 seasonal flavors. At time of posting, the Capitol Hill location offered the following ice creams:

  • Always Flavors
    • Vanilla Bean
    • Melted Chocolate
    • Strawberry
    • Scout Mint
    • Honey Lavender
    • Salted Caramel
    • Earl Grey
    • Stumptown Coffee
    • Maple Walnut
    • Hillary Rodham Cookie Dough
  • Seasonal Flavors
    • Cinnamon
    • Raspberry RGB Sorbet
    • Ginger
    • Vegan Melted Chocolate
Pictured on left are Molly Moon's ginger and salted caramel ice creams. These servings were very affordable at around $5 per cup.
